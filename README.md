

# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis: MySQL to Tableau Pipeline

## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of a year's worth of pizza sales data. I used **MySQL** to query and clean the raw data and **Tableau** to build an interactive story that identifies key trends, top-selling products, and actionable business insights.

## 📊 Key Insights (The "So What?")
*   **Total Revenue:** $817.860
*   **Best Seller:** **Thai Chicken** is the star performer, contributing 21.65% to individual sales.
*   **Underperformer:** The **Brie Carre** pizza is significantly underperforming compared to the rest of the menu.
*   **Upsell Opportunity:** Large pizzas are the most popular size; a strategy to convert Medium buyers to Large could boost margins.

## 💡 Business Recommendations
1.  **Menu Engineering:** Replace or "re-brand" the Brie Carre pizza due to low sales volume.
2.  **Marketing:** Focus "Classic" category promotions on weekends to maximize high-volume periods.
3.  **Operations:** Optimize staffing levels during the peak lunch and dinner hours identified in the hourly trend analysis.
